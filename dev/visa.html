Visa

VISA CLEAR DIGITAL OUTDOOR

Visa tasked me to create a campaign for their Visa Signature CLEAR Card Program.

Appearing as animated digital billboards, static billboards, social media, banner ads, CRM, and a microsite, we used visually arresting imagery to show how Visa CLEAR is revolutionizing air travel by getting you through airport security lines faster. So you can spend more time where it matters most.

VISA CLEAR ANIMATED BILLBOARDS

Billboards were displayed in 11 airports across the country. By incorporating a "time sweep" wipe, we highlighted the ease of transitioning from where you want be, to where you need to be.
