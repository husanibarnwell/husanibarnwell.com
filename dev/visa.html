VISA CHECKOUT CREDIT OFFER

Visa Checkout tasked me with creating awareness for their Universal Credit Statement Offer using non-traditional, digital and social channels. Rather than promote the standard offer of a "$10 credit for new Visa Checkout enrollees," I incorporated Kickstarter and Tinder in an effort to create "digital buzz":

"Pay It Forward" — Kickstarter

With a wink and smile, I reinvented the standard enrollee offer by creating a Kickstarter campaign that flipped-the-script on donating.

With a $1M cap, Visa's offer would award a $10 credit to the first 100,000 enrollees. Therefore, instead of asking for donations, Visa would donate $10 to whomever enrolled in Visa Checkout.

"Score" In-App Advertising — Tinder

Once again, I strove to utilize non-traditional social means to promote Visa Checkout's $10 credit offer to new enrollees — creating a humorous, tongue-in-cheek, in-app engagement to attract our older millennial demographic.

The easier way to pay online presents an easier way to get "hooked-up."

Using Tinder, I created a monetary version of Alexander Hamilton. Swiping right, would allow the user to enroll and receive their $10 credit.

VISA / NFL E-Z SCOREBOARD (aka "BANNER INTERCEPTION")
Browser Plugin / Extension

Tasked with promoting Visa Checkout's sponsorship of the 2015 NFL season, I went beyond a mere digital play and developed an entirely new advertising platform — bringing users up-to-the-minute news, scores, and team highlights, in the places you’d least expect it — banner ads.

Available on Mozilla and Google's Chrome store, this downloadable browser extension would automatically change banner ads on any website into a customizable, Visa Checkout-branded, sports platform — providing ongoing utility that extends far beyond mere adblocking.

"The easier way to pay online gives you an easier way to follow your favorite teams."
