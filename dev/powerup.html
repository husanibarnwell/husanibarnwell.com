PowerUp Fitness

Digital Product Design featuring Internet of Things + Augmented Reality

With PowerUp, surpass your strength goals by seamlessly tracking your reps, weight, speed and power automatically. View Augmented Reality Form demos of your favorite lifts in real time, so you can perfect your technique. Compete against yourself or other PowerUp gyms around the country, seamlessly. Just start up the app and train in your PowerUp gear at PowerUp-equipped gyms. PowerUp helps you put the work in to lifting, and not into charting your progress.
